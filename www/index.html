Extreme Winter Drought Conditions have Increased Wildfire Danger Statewide. Protect your property by creating and maintaining defensible space now.

ChecklistOpen in Todoist

1 Remove all dead plants, grass and weeds (vegetation).

2 Remove dead or dry leaves and pine needles from your yard, roof and gutters.

3 Trim trees regularly to keep branches a minimum of 10 feet from other trees.

4 Remove branches that hang over your roof and keep dead branches 10 feet away.

5 Relocate wood piles into Zone 2.

6 Remove or prune flammable plants and shrubs near windows.

7 Create a separation between trees, shrubs and items that could catch fire.
